About Mitchell Starc

Mitchell Aaron Starc is an Australian international cricketer who plays for the Australian national team and New South Wales in domestic cricket. A left-arm fast bowler and a lower order left-handed batsman, he is Australia's fourth highest wicket-taker in both Test and One Day International cricket.

Date of Birth: 30-Jan-1990

Nationality: Australian

Profession: Cricketer






Achievements

  • He was named the Player of the Tournament in the 2015 ODI World Cup.
  • Starc is the fastest bowler in ODI history to reach 200 wickets
  • He bowled the fastest delivery in Test cricket history at a blistering 160.4 km/h against New Zealand in 2015.
